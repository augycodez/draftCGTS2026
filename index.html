About the Symposium
A Decade of Progress.
A Future of Cures.

Now in its 11th year, the Cell and Gene Therapy Symposium has become a leading forum for CGT professionals across India and internationally. Since its launch in 2015 with just 80 participants, the symposium has evolved into a flagship event that attracts global leaders and innovators in viral vectors, CAR-T therapies, gene editing, and advanced manufacturing.

Our mission is to fast-track the journey from groundbreaking scientific discovery to transformative therapies for patients with rare and complex diseases. Through high-impact scientific sessions, interactive workshops, and purposeful networking opportunities, CGTS 2026 will further advance the frontiers of curative medicine.

Scientific Programme
Core Themes 2026

This year's symposium is structured around six major scientific themes, each reflecting the most dynamic and clinically impactful areas in cell and gene therapy.

CRISPR & Base Editing

Advances in precision genome editing, base editors, prime editors, and epigenome targeting for monogenic and complex diseases.

CAR-T & Cell Therapies

Next-generation CAR constructs, allogeneic platforms, NK cell therapies, TIL therapy, and overcoming solid tumor resistance.

Viral Vector Delivery

AAV capsid engineering, lentiviral optimization, novel tropism, immune evasion strategies, and scalable GMP manufacturing.

Non-Viral Delivery

Lipid nanoparticles, polymeric nanocarriers, exosome-based delivery, and electroporation advances enabling safer gene transfer.

Regulatory & Manufacturing

FDA and EMA pathways, accelerated approval designations, GMP compliance, cost-of-goods challenges, and commercial-scale production.

Patient Access & Ethics

Health economics, equitable access, informed consent in early-phase trials, germline editing ethics, and global policy frameworks.
